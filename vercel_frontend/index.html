How It Works

01

Camera Capture

Webcam continuously captures video frames of the driver's face in real-time.

02

Face & Eye Detection

Haar cascade classifiers detect faces and eyes within each video frame.

03

State Analysis

System tracks eye closure duration and frequency to determine drowsiness level.

04

Alert Activation

When drowsiness is detected, sound, SMS, email, and call alerts are triggered immediately.
